Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

Synapse ("we", "our", "the app") is operated by Main Loop Systems LLC. This policy describes how Synapse accesses, uses, stores, and protects your data when you use the hosted cloud version of Synapse connected to your Google Drive.

1. What data we access

Synapse requests the drive.file OAuth scope from Google. This is a limited, non-sensitive scope that allows Synapse to:

  • Create new files and folders in your Google Drive (your Synapse vault)
  • Read and modify files that you explicitly select using the Google Picker

Synapse cannot browse, search, or access any files in your Google Drive that you have not explicitly selected or that the app has not created. We do not request broad read access to your Drive.

2. How we use your data

Files you select or create through Synapse are used solely to provide the Synapse knowledge base service:

  • Reading files to respond to queries through your AI client (Claude, ChatGPT, Cursor, etc.)
  • Creating compiled summaries, wiki pages, and organized notes within your vault
  • Copying files you select into your Synapse vault folder during the import process

We do not use your files for training AI models, advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond providing the Synapse service to you.

3. Data storage and transmission

  • Your files stay in Google Drive. Synapse does not download or store copies of your files on our servers. All file operations happen through the Google Drive API.
  • OAuth tokens (access tokens and refresh tokens) are stored in server memory for the duration of your session. They are not persisted to disk or a database. When our server restarts, all tokens are cleared and you must re-authenticate.
  • No personal information (name, email, profile data) is collected or stored by Synapse beyond what Google provides in the OAuth token.
  • Server infrastructure is hosted on Railway (railway.app) in the United States.

4. Data sharing

We do not sell, share, or transfer your data to any third parties. Your files are accessed only through the Google Drive API in response to your requests via your chosen AI client.

5. Data retention and deletion

  • Session data: OAuth tokens exist only in server memory and are automatically cleared on server restart (typically within 24 hours).
  • Vault files: Files created by Synapse in your Google Drive are owned by you and persist in your Drive until you delete them.
  • Account deletion: You can revoke Synapse's access at any time by visiting myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing "Synapse Cloud." This immediately revokes all access. No data remains on our servers to delete.

6. Security

  • All communication between your browser, our server, and Google's APIs uses HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • OAuth tokens are never logged, written to disk, or exposed in URLs.
  • The Google Picker API key used client-side is restricted to the Google Picker API only and cannot be used to access Drive data directly.
  • Synapse is open source. You can inspect the code at github.com/tomjrworks/synapse-obsidian.

7. Children's privacy

Synapse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of Synapse after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or to request data deletion, contact us at tom@mainloopsystems.com.
