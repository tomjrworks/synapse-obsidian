How It Works

Four operations, one growing brain

Synapse connects your AI to your notes. Every interaction makes the knowledge base smarter.

01 — Save

Capture anything

Save articles, research, ideas from any device. Share a URL or paste content.

“Save this article to my vault: https://example.com/ai-trends”

02 — Compile

Build the wiki

Raw sources become structured knowledge — summaries, concept pages, entity pages, all cross-linked.

“Compile my knowledge base”

03 — Query

Ask questions

Research across everything you’ve saved. Get cited answers. Every answer feeds back into the wiki.

“What do my notes say about pricing strategy?”

04 — Maintain

Health check

Find contradictions, broken links, orphan pages, stale content. Fix what it can, flag the rest.

“Run a health check on my vault”
